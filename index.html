The story of me, a woman trying to bring the monies back

Not much had happened lately in her life. With the two bootcamps that she was doing and the slight knee pain she was feeling, it seems fun times were near null. But, happily she had accepted that sedentary life, nobody would get in between a lady with will and her career goals.

Was she scared? Yes. Was she having no sex life? That too. Was she like an alien to the world of partying and traveling where she was supposed to fit in by being far from what had been home for 11 years? For sure. Did she miss hiking in Northern California? Absolutely. But, what was there to do, giving up? Enough said, never happening.

As she thought and thought more about how isolating and how tight her budget was under this process, she figured enjoyment will come later. One day, she will finally weep her tears with 100 dollar bills and have that debt swoop by her own merit, she would resume hoping place to place as a digital nomad and renew her spirits under a better work environment.
